Our Story

Founded in 1998, AutoDealer began as a small family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable automobile sales and service to our community. What started with just 20 vehicles has grown into one of the region's most trusted dealerships.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers annually, offering an extensive selection of new and certified pre-owned vehicles from top manufacturers. Our commitment to transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction has remained unchanged throughout our growth.

With state-of-the-art facilities, ASE-certified technicians, and a customer-first approach, we continue to set the standard for automotive excellence in our community.
